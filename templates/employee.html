Website Administration

Content Management System

The CMS will help authorized website administrators manage regular updates, publications and media without depending on code-level changes.

Module Included Capability
News Management Create, update and publish company news and announcements.
Publications Management Manage research reports, market updates and other downloadable publications.
Banner Management Update homepage banners and key promotional website content.
Downloads Upload and organize forms, documents, circulars and client resources.
User Management Manage authorized CMS users and content publishing access.
Media Library Store and reuse images, documents and website media assets.
Homepage Editor Update selected homepage sections for timely investor communication.
